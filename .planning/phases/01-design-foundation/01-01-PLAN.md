1. **Update @custom-variant directive (line 8-9):**

Change from:

```css
@custom-variant dark (&:is(.dark *));
@custom-variant light (&:is(:not(.dark) *));
```

To:

```css
@custom-variant dark (&:is(:not(.light) *));
@custom-variant light (&:is(.light *));
```
